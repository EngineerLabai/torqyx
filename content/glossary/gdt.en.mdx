GD&T is geometric dimensioning and tolerancing.

## Why it matters

It defines form, orientation, location, and runout requirements by function.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
Position tolerance controls hole location more clearly than simple plus-minus dimensions.
</Callout>
