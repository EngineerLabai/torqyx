Hardness is resistance to indentation or scratching.

## Why it matters

It is a quick indicator for wear, machinability, and heat treatment control.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
A high HRC tool steel may resist wear but can become more brittle.
</Callout>
