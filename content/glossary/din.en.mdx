DIN is a family of standards published by the German standards body.

## Why it matters

It is common in machine elements, fasteners, and manufacturing details.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
DIN bolt and washer standards are common in European supply chains.
</Callout>
