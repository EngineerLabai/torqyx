A datum is a reference surface, axis, or point for measurement and tolerances.

## Why it matters

It defines assembly function and measurement repeatability.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
The main seating face in a fixture is often the primary datum.
</Callout>
