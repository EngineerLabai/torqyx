ISO is an organization that develops international standards.

## Why it matters

It provides common language for dimensions, quality, materials, and management systems.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.

ISO 286 is a common reference for limits and fits.
