CNC means computer numerical control of a machine tool.

## Why it matters

It enables repeatable dimensions, complex tool paths, and production runs.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
In CNC milling, tool paths can be generated with CAM software.
</Callout>
