ASTM publishes material, test method, and product standards.

## Why it matters

It is often used when interpreting material certificates and test procedures.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
ASTM A36 is a well-known structural steel material standard.
