Resonance is vibration amplification when excitation frequency approaches natural frequency.

## Why it matters

It increases fatigue risk in rotating equipment, frames, and joints.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
If a fan suddenly vibrates at a certain speed, it may be near resonance.
</Callout>
