Clearance is the positive space between two parts.

## Why it matters

It can be required for rotation, sliding, assembly, and thermal expansion.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
If the shaft is smaller than the hole, the difference is clearance.
</Callout>
