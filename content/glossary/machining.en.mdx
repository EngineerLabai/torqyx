Machining shapes material by removing chips with cutting tools.

## Why it matters

It is essential for tolerance, surface finish, and prototype production.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.

Turning and milling are common machining examples.
