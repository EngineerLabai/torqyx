Additive manufacturing builds a part layer by layer by adding material.

## Why it matters

It helps prototypes, complex internal channels, and low-volume production.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.
