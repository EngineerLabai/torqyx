Forging plastically shapes solid metal under pressure.

## Why it matters

It is used for high-strength parts because of grain flow and dense structure.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
Crankshafts and connecting rods can be forged parts.
</Callout>
