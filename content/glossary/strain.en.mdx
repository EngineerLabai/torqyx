Strain is the change in length divided by original length.

## Why it matters

It helps identify whether a material works in the elastic or plastic region.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Formula label="Basic expression">
Strain = delta L / L0
</Formula>

<Callout type="info" title="Example">
A 1 m length that extends by 1 mm has strain of 0.001.
</Callout>
