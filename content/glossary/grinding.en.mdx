Grinding removes material with abrasive grains for precision finishing.

## Why it matters

It is used for hard parts, tight tolerances, and good surface finish.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
Bearing seat surfaces can be precision ground.
