ASME is known for mechanical engineering and pressure equipment standards.

## Why it matters

It can be a reference for boilers, pressure vessels, piping, and GD&T.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
ASME Y14.5 is a common source for geometric tolerancing.
</Callout>
