Casting forms parts by pouring molten material into a mold.

## Why it matters

It is useful for complex shapes and production volumes.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
Pump housings and engine blocks are common casting examples.
</Callout>
