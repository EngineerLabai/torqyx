Rolling reduces thickness or forms material by passing it through rolls.

## Why it matters

It is a common base process for sheet, profile, and bar production.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
Hot rolling affects structure; cold rolling affects dimension and surface quality.
</Callout>
