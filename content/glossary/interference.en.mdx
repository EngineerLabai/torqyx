Interference is dimensional overlap before assembly.

## Why it matters

It is used for torque and force transfer in press fits.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
If the shaft is larger than the hole, pressing or thermal assembly is needed.
</Callout>
