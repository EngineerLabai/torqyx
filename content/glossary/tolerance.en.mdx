Tolerance is the acceptable variation range of a dimension.

## Why it matters

It directly affects manufacturability, assembly clearance, and cost.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
A 25.00 plus minus 0.02 mm dimension has a total tolerance range of 0.04 mm.
</Callout>
