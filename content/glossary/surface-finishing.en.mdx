Surface finishing is the family of final processes that change surface properties.

## Why it matters

It affects corrosion, friction, appearance, and cleanability.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
Anodizing, galvanizing, paint, and passivation are common examples.
</Callout>
