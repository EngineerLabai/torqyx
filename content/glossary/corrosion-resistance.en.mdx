Corrosion resistance is resistance to chemical or electrochemical degradation.

## Why it matters

It controls life-cycle cost in outdoor, marine, and chemical service.

## How it is used

In a design check, first define the relevant load, dimension, material, or flow condition. Then interpret this term by its role in the formula and compare it with limits.


<Callout type="info" title="Example">
316 stainless can be safer than 304 in chloride service.
</Callout>
