## Core steps in calculation workflows

Accurate engineering requires following a calculation workflow step by step. Clear definition of each stage reduces the risk of mistakes.

## Example workflow

1. Define the problem.
2. Collect data.
3. Choose formulas.
4. Perform the calculation.
5. Verify the result.

## Weekly sharing suggestion

Each week, cover one part of the workflow, for example:

- Checking initial data
- Verifying unit conversions
- Applying safety factors

## Outcome

This style of weekly content gives readers consistent value and makes your blog easier to discover by category.
