## Why title selection matters

The right title helps your content reach the target audience. In engineering-focused blog posts, clear and specific titles work best.

## How to choose a title

- Define the main idea of the article.
- Identify key terms.
- Keep the title short and descriptive.
- Make sure it answers the reader's question.

## Sample title sets

- "Weekly torque calculation checklist"
- "What to consider when choosing content titles"
- "Clear topic titles for engineering blog posts"

## Categories and tags

Categories help group topics. Tags help filter articles by more detailed keywords.

## Conclusion

Every new post should connect to previous content and make it easy for readers to follow.
