## Why publish weekly?

Weekly blog posts provide readers with a steady stream of updates and help keep your site fresh. Each week, you can explore one new topic in depth.

## Planning steps

1. Create a publishing calendar.
2. Define each week's subject in advance.
3. Group topics under a clear heading.
4. Clarify the content outline and target message.

## Choosing a topic

- A technical calculation step
- An engineering trend
- A project management tip
- A software or prototyping workflow

## Example titles

- "Weekly calculation checklist"
- "Reliable data input process in engineering"
- "Better blog management with topic headings"

## Tip

Using categories and tags on each post helps visitors find related content more easily.
